Purpose

The Flower Mound Chamber of Commerce helps established business stay strong and aspiring entrepreneurs get strong.
We contribute meaningfully to our community through uniting local businesses in service projects and through civic engagement.
This site fulfills this purpose with these pages:

Home
This page delivers a condensed purpose message and an invitation to join. It also highlights recent successed from local business owners.
Discover
We want to draw new businesses to our city. This page will house important information about demographics such as average annual income, gender, and age. Businesses will be able to see the value of doing business in Flower Mound through the use of attractive charts and maps.
Directory
A list of current members and their contact information.
Join
All pages lead here for new visitors. This page will have an application form.
Contact
This page will house a contact form. To cut down on unnecessary correspondence, a side bar of FAQs will be present.

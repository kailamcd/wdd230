Target Audience

This site will be used to attract and serve business owners in Flower Mound. They are chiefly over 40 years old and want to strengthen their business through civic engagement and networking.
Visitors will use both desktops and cell phones to view the site. May be higher traffic during business hours.

Scenarios and Personas

Visitors will have a few key concerns when they visit this site:

New to the Area/Considering a Move
Jeremy Smith, an entrepreneur looking to expand his clothing boutique franchises, is looking at Flower Mound for a brick and mortar presence. He'll need to know if his target demographic is represented in Flower Mound.
Networking
Wilhemina Marks, a Real Estate Agent wants to find a reliable Plumber to recommend. She'll be to meet local plumbers and find a good fit through this site.
Starting a Business
Felicity Davis is starting her first business. It will be valuable to her to explore the directory and find out who her competitors will be. She will also be able to learn about the community and find ways to get the word out to potential customers.
Civic Engagement
Henry Phillips is concerned about local legislation's new proposals and how they'll impact his business. He can find information on what new laws include and when to vote here.
Community Impact
Sam Villasenor wants to set up a team service project for his employees. He can find other companies to partner with to increase their impact.

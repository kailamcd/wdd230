Target Audience

This site will be used to attract and serve business owners in Flower Mound. They are chiefly over 40 years old and want to strengthen their business through civic engagement and networking.
Visitors will use both desktops and cell phones to view the site. May be higher traffic during business hours.

Scenarios

Visitors will have a few key concerns when they visit this site:

New to the Area/Considering a Move
If an owner is looking at Flower Mound for a brick and mortar presence, they'll need to know what Flower Mound has to offer.
Networking
Whether a Real Estate Agent wants to find a reliable Plumber to recommend or a bakery wants to find out if there are any local fruit distributors, visitors should be able to find what they're looking for on this site.
Starting a Business
As a person becomes an entrepreneurs, it will be valuable to explore the directory to see who their competitors are. They will also be able to learn about the community and find ways to meet potential customers.
